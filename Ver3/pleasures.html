III·Rituals of the house

Little luxuries.

Not amenities for a brochure — the actual things that make a stay feel like itself. A bar you'd come back to. A gym run by people who care. A desk when you need one.

Explore
III · 01 — Social

Bar & social lounge

Hungarian vermouths, natural wines from Tokaj, classics made slowly. Not a hotel bar — a bar that happens to be in a hotel. It spills into the Twenty Six courtyard every evening after five, and stays open as long as it needs to.

The cocktail list is short and seasonal. The bar team knows what they're doing. You can bring your laptop if you're not ready to be social yet.

Opens17:00 daily
StyleCraft cocktails · Natural wine
LocationGround floor, Twenty Six
TerraceInner courtyard, seasonal
III · 02 — Body

Gym, with GoActive

We don't run the gym ourselves — our neighbours at GoActive do, and they do it better than we could. Full Technogym equipment, a sauna, and a team that knows the names of regulars. Complimentary with every stay.

A five-minute walk from the hotel. Classes available on the schedule below — book ahead, they fill quickly.

Hours06:00 — 22:00
EquipmentFull Technogym suite
SaunaIncluded
CostComplimentary
III · 03 — Work

Create 26 coworking

A quiet floor of private desks and meeting rooms shared with the Twenty Six building community. One desk per guest, at no charge. Good wifi, good coffee, enough quiet to think.

For longer stays, or for guests who work as they travel — a place that takes work seriously without being precious about it.

Hours08:00 — 20:00
DesksOne per guest
MeetingsBookable rooms
CostComplimentary
