II·Six rooms, six temperaments

Find your room.

Each room carries its own tone — choose one and the house takes that colour. From a two-floor Presidential with private sauna to a Superior that is cosy on purpose.

I01 / 06

Presidential Suite

A two-floor sanctuary at the top of the house. King bedroom upstairs, living room and dining downstairs, with a private Jacuzzi and sauna tucked behind a hidden door.

II02 / 06

King Suite with Jacuzzi

A double-height suite with a real Jacuzzi, a sauna, and a green-wall rain shower. The kind of room that quietly tells you to stay in tonight.

III03 / 06

Junior Suite

Living room, sleeping area, sofa-bed for a third. A whole apartment’s worth of room, with art chosen for it specifically.

IV04 / 06

Deluxe Room

A king-bed room with a small living corner and original commissioned art. Generous on purpose — not a category to apologise for.

V05 / 06

Deluxe Queen

Queen bed, inner-courtyard windows, quiet. The room for travellers who plan to be out — and want sleep when they’re back.

VI06 / 06

Superior Room

Cosy on purpose. A queen bed, a working desk, a monochrome tile feature wall. Honest about what 17 m² can be when it’s done with care.
