Questions, answered.

Everything guests usually want to know before they arrive. If yours isn't here, write to us — we're quick.

Arrival & stay

What are the check-in and check-out times?+

Check-in is from 15:00. Check-out is by 11:00. If you're arriving earlier or need a late checkout, tell us when you book — we'll do what we can. Direct bookings include late checkout subject to availability.

Is there someone at the hotel around the clock?+

Yes. There's always someone here between 15:00 and 22:00 for check-ins. For arrivals outside those hours, we arrange a keybox or a team member will meet you — just let us know in advance.

Can I store luggage before check-in or after check-out?+

Yes, luggage storage is available on request. Drop it off and go explore — we'll look after it.

What is included in the stay?+

All room rates include daily breakfast at Twenty Six restaurant, access to the GoActive gym and sauna, access to Create 26 coworking, and high-speed WiFi throughout the building. Direct bookings also receive a complimentary welcome drink at the bar.

The rooms

How many rooms are in the hotel?+

Six rooms, each with its own number and character — from the Presidential Suite (165–180 m², two floors) to the Superior Room (17 m², deliberately cosy). See the Rooms page for the full list.

Do any rooms have a Jacuzzi or sauna?+

Yes. The Presidential Suite and King Suite both have private Jacuzzis. The Presidential Suite also has a private sauna. All guests have access to the GoActive gym sauna regardless of room type.

Can rooms accommodate more than two guests?+

The Junior Suite sleeps up to three with its sofa-bed arrangement. The Presidential Suite accommodates four comfortably. Please enquire for specific room configurations.

Are pets allowed?+

We love the idea but the building doesn't allow it. We're sorry.

Food & drink

What time is breakfast served?+

07:00 to 11:00, Monday through Sunday, in the Twenty Six restaurant on the ground floor. If you have an early flight or need something before 07:00, speak to us the night before.

Is breakfast included for all guests?+

Yes, breakfast is included in all room rates. It's à la carte — you order what you want, not what's left.

When does the bar open?+

The bar opens at 17:00 daily and closes when it should. On warm evenings it spills into the Twenty Six courtyard. Guests are welcome to use the bar at any time — it's your living room too.

Can you accommodate dietary requirements?+

Yes. Let us know when you book and the kitchen will prepare for it. The menu has options for most dietary needs — vegetarian, gluten-free, dairy-free. Just ask.

Access & location

How do I get to the hotel from the airport?+

Budapest Liszt Ferenc Airport is about 35 minutes by taxi (fixed rate of approximately 9,000 Ft). The 100E airport express bus goes to Deák tér, from where it's a 15-minute walk or a short tram ride. We can arrange a private transfer if you'd prefer — just ask.

Is there parking at the hotel?+

We don't have a dedicated car park, but there are paid parking zones on and around Király utca, and a public car park on Akácfa utca a short walk away. We'll send details when you book.

Is the hotel accessible for guests with mobility needs?+

The building is a historic townhouse with some limitations. The ground floor and common areas are step-free, but some rooms require using stairs. Please contact us before booking and we'll advise honestly.

Booking & policies

What is the cancellation policy?+

Free cancellation up to 48 hours before arrival. Cancellations within 48 hours are charged one night. No-shows are charged the full stay. We can make exceptions for genuine emergencies — just call us.

Why book directly rather than through an OTA?+

Direct bookings get a complimentary welcome drink at the bar and late checkout (subject to availability). We also know who you are before you arrive, which means we can prepare better. The rate is the same — or sometimes better.

Do you accept group bookings?+

With six rooms, we can accommodate a whole-house buyout — which is occasionally how guests celebrate something significant. Get in touch and we'll talk through what's possible.

Is there a minimum stay?+

No minimum stay as a rule, though some periods (long weekends, major events) may have a two-night minimum. The booking form will show what applies to your dates.

Still have a question?

Write to us or call. We're a small team and we read everything — usually within a few hours.
